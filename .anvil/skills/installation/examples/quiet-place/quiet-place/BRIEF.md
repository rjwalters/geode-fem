# Brief

A five-meter geodesic sphere lined with anechoic wedge foam, suspended or floor-mounted in a public atrium or gallery. Two strangers enter through opposing airlocks; the inner shell captures a silence deeper than a professional recording studio. They have one minute together in near-total acoustic isolation. A gong ends the session. The piece is participatory and durational — the work happens in the encounter between two people who do not know each other, inside a silence they did not expect.
