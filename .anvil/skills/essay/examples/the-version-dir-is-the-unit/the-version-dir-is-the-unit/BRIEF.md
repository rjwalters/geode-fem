# Brief

A short essay arguing that the immutable version directory — not the file,
not the commit — is the right atomic unit for an AI authoring loop. Ground
the voice against the project `corpus/` exemplars (`exemplar-on-iteration.md`,

declared in the project `voice:` block. Open inside a concrete moment, keep
every paragraph load-bearing, and land on a short declarative close per the
style guide. 500–1500 words, markdown body named for the slug.
